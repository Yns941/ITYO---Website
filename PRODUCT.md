# ITYO — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Landing site for ITYO, a French AI automation agency based in Paris. Converts prospects into leads by demonstrating expertise, credibility, and urgency around business automation.

## Users
French PME/TPE owners, startups, and decision-makers aged 30-55 who are time-poor, skeptical of tech jargon, and motivated by ROI and operational efficiency. They visit on desktop, during business hours, looking for a partner who understands their pain — not a tech vendor.

## Register
Brand — marketing site, design IS the product.

## Brand Personality
Luxury-meets-precision. Like a high-end consulting firm that actually ships. Not startup-cool, not enterprise-bland. Think Obsidian & Gold: serious, ambitious, refined. The gold is not decoration — it signals value and transformation.

## Tone
Direct, confident, slightly provocative. Uses imperative. No jargon, no buzzwords. French copy that respects the reader's intelligence.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS purple gradients
- Glassmorphism
- "AI startup" neon-on-black aesthetic
- Soft pastel consulting sites
- Template-looking layouts

## Strategic Principles
- Urgency without panic: frame automation delay as a competitive risk, not a fear tactic
- Social proof through specificity: real project names, real metrics
- Hierarchy: Contact/CTA is the real conversion goal — everything leads there
- One job per section — no combined sections that dilute message
